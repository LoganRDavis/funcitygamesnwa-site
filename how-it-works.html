Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions we get on almost every consultation call.

How much space do I need?

As little as a single claw machine footprint (about 3×3 feet) up to a full arcade corner. We'll recommend a lineup that fits your space and traffic.

Do I pay anything upfront?

Never. We cover equipment, delivery, installation, prizes, service, repairs, and insurance. You just provide the outlet and the square footage.

What's the profit split?

It varies by machine type and location, but it's always transparent and agreed upfront. We'll walk you through exact numbers during our free consultation.

What if a game isn't making money?

We swap it. Plain and simple. We want every machine on our route earning, so our incentives line up with yours.

Am I locked in?

No. If it's not working for your business, we pick the games up. We'd rather keep earning partners than force bad fits.
