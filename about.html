We Love Games. We Love Small Business.

Most businesses have a spot that isn't pulling its weight. A corner near the door, a stretch of waiting area, an empty lobby. Put the right machine there and everyone wins.

Since 2010, we've placed and serviced claw machines, arcade cabinets, redemption games, jukeboxes, pool tables, and bulk vending machines all across Northwest Arkansas, the Fort Smith area, and Eastern Oklahoma. Our partners are restaurants, bars, breweries, laundromats, car washes, and family entertainment spots, the businesses that make up everyday life out here.

Fun City Games is owned and operated by Zacc Arnold, who runs the route out of NWA. We live here, our trucks are based here, and our service techs are folks you might run into at the grocery store. When you call us, a real person picks up.
