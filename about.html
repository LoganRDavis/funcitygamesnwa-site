About Fun City Games

A Northwest Arkansas arcade route built on fair profit splits, dependable service, and games that actually make money.

We Love Games. We Love Small Business.

Fun City Games started with a simple idea: every good business has a spot somewhere — a corner, a waiting area, a lobby — that could be doing more. Put the right arcade machine there and everyone wins.

Since 2010, we've been placing and servicing claw machines, arcade cabinets, and bulk vending machines with local restaurants, breweries, laundromats, car washes, and family entertainment spots all across Northwest Arkansas.

We're local. Our trucks are local. Our service team is local. And when you call us, a real person answers.
